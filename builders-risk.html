What Is Builder's Risk Insurance?

Builder's risk insurance covers buildings and structures under construction or renovation. It protects against losses from fire, theft, vandalism, windstorm, and other covered perils during the construction period. Whether you're a developer, contractor, or property owner, this coverage is essential.

What's Typically Covered

  • Damage to the structure under construction
  • Theft of materials and equipment on-site
  • Fire, wind, hail, and other weather events
  • Vandalism and malicious mischief
  • Scaffolding and temporary structures
  • Soft costs coverage available
